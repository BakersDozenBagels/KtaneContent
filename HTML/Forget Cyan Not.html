Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Forget Cyan Not

On the Subject of Forget Cyan Not

“This module is very simple that even babies can do it!” -Cyanix 2023

This module has two screens, a main display on the bottom, a stage counter on the right, and ten buttons numbered 0–9.

Upon every solve of a non-ignored module, take the two numbers at the bottom display and add them together. This will be the calculated digit for that stage.

Once all non-ignored modules are solved, press the calculated digits in the order they appeared. Once all the digits have been inputted, the module will disarm.

If an incorrect digit is inputted, a strike will be incurred, and one or two LEDs will light up on the buttons. If two LEDs light up, the two numbers that lit up were the two numbers displayed at that stage. If one LED lights up, the one number that lit up were both numbers displayed at that stage.

Receiving a strike will require you to input the full sequence from the start.
