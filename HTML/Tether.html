Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Tether

On the Subject of Tether

Together, they are stronger. You need to unbind their links.

The module consists of a 4 x 4 chessboard with numbers on each of the tiles. The white tiles normally seen on a chess board have been replaced with red.

Pressing a tile on the board will change 7-9 numbers at different coordinates. Each of those numbers will change color to white when this happens. The numbers that will be added to those numbers are between 1 and 9. If a number goes above 10, the number will be modulo 10.

Using the tiles from the board, find a way so that each number on the board will be equal to each other.
