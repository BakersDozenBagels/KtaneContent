Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Yellow Button't

On the Subject of Yellow Button't

Yellow cube button.

On the module, there is a yellow button and a screen.

Get the number of the teeth of each gear:

Z1 = W × R + M + W
Z2 = Z1 × R + M + W
Z3 = Z2 × R + M + W
(rounding down)...

Where R is equal to the number of seconds it takes for the button to rotate 360 degrees, M is equal to the number on the display, and W is equal to the number on the button. This means that you need to do W×2 times.

In this module there are W gears. To solve this module, cancel out the torque of the gear rotation, which can be done by tapping the button at a special time(input torque % 60(rounding down)).

Get the answer:

Input torque is equal to the sum of all gear teeth + M.

Note: If you don't have a way to get the number of seconds of rotation, the display will be empty whenever the rotation starts, and when it lights up again at the end, you can get R by counting the number of seconds the display does not show.
