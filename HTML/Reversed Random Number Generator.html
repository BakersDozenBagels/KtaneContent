Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Reversed Random Number Generator

On the Subject of Reversed Random Number Generator

Simply use your free will.

When this module activates, it will begin a countdown. There is no way to deactivate it. However, you can increase the timer by 2 seconds by pressing either button (the corresponding keys on your keyboard work, too).

However, before each press is made, the module will predict your next press. If it is correct, the press will subtract 1 second from the timer instead of adding.

It is recommended that you prepare a truly random sequence of presses in advance of when you will need them.
