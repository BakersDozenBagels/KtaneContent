Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Watch the Clock

On the Subject of Watch the Clock

This is a module that penalizes bomb interaction while the last digit of the bomb’s timer matches the digit displayed on the module.

There is a large numerical digit (the “Digit”) displayed on the left half of any Watch the Clock module (the “Module”) on any bomb (the “Bomb”). Any interaction* within the Bomb that occurs while the last digit of the Bomb’s timer (the “Timer”) matches the Digit will incur a strike against the Module. There is also a red button (the “Button”) on the right side of the Module. Interacting with the Button will not incur a strike. Interacting with the Button will increment the Digit by one (1).** While the Digit appears cyan and/or displays the letter “E”, interacting with the Button will solve the Module.

If there are multiple Watch the Clock modules on a given bomb, they may each have their own digit displayed, but breaking any of their rules will incur a strike somewhere or somehow.

*Interaction involves pressing a selectable object within a bomb module. Rotating the bomb or selecting and deselecting modules themselves can be done freely.

**Cycling the digit is useful when another module requires the timer to end in a particular digit, e.g. The Button.
